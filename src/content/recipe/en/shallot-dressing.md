## Ingredients

- Coarsely chopped shallots
- Neutral oil
- Red wine vinegar
- Dijon mustard

## Method

1. Blend, pour in oil at a steady pace and salt to taste.

### Note
Dressing will separate due to not being an emulsion. Just give it a shake before using.
